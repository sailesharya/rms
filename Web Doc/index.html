 INTRODUCTION

ProcessMAP Recruitment management software is more than a recruitment database, it parses emails, manages and creates documents, enters data, synchronizes with Social Networking sites , emails applicants, and synchronizes with your calendar and job seeker websites like Monster and Naukri -all automatically. Recruitment Management System offers the most powerful start-to-finish tools for managing your placement ratio. Rather than just focusing on candidates, it gives your recruiters the tools to effectively carry out the recruiting procedure.


 SCOPE

  • Online system to track recruitment process at one place: This software offers the most powerful start-to-finish tool that not only focuses on candidates,but also gives recruiters the power to effectively carry out the recruiting procedure. Various users can log-in into the software and will have the rights predefined by admin according to the user roles.

  • Feature to submit online application with resume: This software also provide feature to submit online application. As soon as the candidate will click on the external link provided at various portals and sites, he will be asked for his name and email-address on clicking the submit button, a link will be sent to his email id through which he can proceed further and apply with the auto-generated user-id.

  • Filtering and shortlisting candidates: Filtering and Short-listing of candidates will be done at HR screening, HR will get the list of candidates applying for a job from which he can short-list the candidates.

  • Supports scheduling and customizable recruitment process: This software supports customizable recruitment Process, that is depending on the requirement or resources HR can customize the rounds and interviews. He will be able to schedule interviews and assign interviewer independently.

  • Collect information show statistics: The software will keep track of all the job vacancies opening and closing along with the employee distribution among various departments and branches of the company. Graphs will be used to depict the company demographics and employee tally.

  • Feature to assign responsibility to team: It will have a feature where responsibilities will be assigned to various users, like HOD will post job vacancies from his department, the management must approve it, after approval from management its HR' s job to post it onto the vacancies' list.

  • Customizable notification: Notification feature will help the users to be updated with the on-going procedures. For example: if HOD posted a new vacancy it will send a notification to the management for review. Similarly when HR will assign interview, the interviewer will receive a notification about the interview which he can confirm or decline.

  • Social network & job portals linking: The Software will give an option to HR to post the job advertisements on various platforms like Career page of the company, Job Portals and Social Networking sites. He will also get option to post on different groups he belongs to. The post will contain a link through which candidates can apply for the job.

  • Referral management: This feature will help all the employees to refer a candidate, they just have to mention their employee -Id, Candidates name, Phone number and Email-address. The candidate will receive a mail which will contain a link to Candidate Form through which he can apply for the job.

  • Analytical Dashboard: The Contents as well as the menu options will be customized based on the user roles. Each user will can view some common contents like Company demographics and Employee Tally.
